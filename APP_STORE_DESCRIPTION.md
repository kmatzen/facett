## Description

Facett lets you control multiple action cameras at once from your iPhone. Connect up to 9 cameras over Bluetooth Low Energy, organize them into groups, synchronize settings, and start or stop recording across every camera simultaneously.

**Key Features**

• Connect and manage multiple cameras over Bluetooth Low Energy
• Organize cameras into reusable groups for coordinated control
• Start and stop recording on all cameras at the same time
• Sync resolution, frame rate, lens, and exposure settings across a group
• Monitor battery level, storage, and recording status in real time
• Voice commands — say "start" or "stop" for hands-free operation
• Generate QR codes for advanced camera configuration
• Auto-reconnect if a camera drops its connection

**Compatibility**

Facett has been tested with GoPro® HERO10 Black cameras running GoPro Labs firmware. Other camera models may work but are not officially supported. GoPro Labs firmware is required for full BLE connectivity.
